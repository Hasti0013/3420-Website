Project

Meet Me Halfway

Meet Me Halfway is a collaborative web application designed to help users find a convenient meeting location between two given points. It simplifies the process of selecting a midpoint location by providing suggestions based on predefined criteria, including distance and venue type (e.g., cafes, restaurants).
